Statistics of AMBER ALERT

Home Preventing Child Abduction Statistics of Amber Alert

This page intends to demonstrate the prevalence of using AMBER Alerts, and by extension, the amount of child abductions occurring.

  1. 2017 - 195 AMBER Alerts - 263 children: 193 recovered, 2 children missing (as of 2018) and 6 deceased
  2. 2016 - 179 AMBER Alerts - 231 children: 155 recovered, 3 children missing (as of 2017) and 13 deceased
  3. 2015 - 182 AMBER Alerts - 224 children: 153 recovered, 2 children missing (as of 2015) and 8 deceased.
  4. 2014 - 186 AMBER Alerts - 239 children: 154 recovered, 6 children missing (as of 2014) and 6 deceased.
  5. 2013 - 194 AMBER Alerts - 243 children: 185 recovered, 3 children missing (as of 2013) and 11 deceased.
  6. 2012 - 167 AMBER Alerts - 204 children: 158 recovered, 2 children missing (as of 2012) and 9 deceased.
  7. 2011 - 158 AMBER Alerts - 197 children: 144 recovered, 5 children missing (as of 2011) and 5 deceased.
  8. 2010 - 173 AMBER Alerts - 211 children: 150 recovered, 2 children missing (as of 2010) and 9 deceased.
  9. This only includes the reported abductions, and does not include the unreported abductions occurring in the United States.
