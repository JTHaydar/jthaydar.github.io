This page will tell you what you and others can do to help prevent the abduction of children.

  1. Teach your kids to stay away from strangers.
  2. Tell your kids that strangers may look kind but their actions may tell you otherwise.
  3. Tell your kids if they are asked for help by someone they do not know go to the police.
  4. When strangers ask for help it can include finding a lost dog.
  5. Kids should trust their own instincts; this includes walking away if they feel uncomfortable.
  6. Tell your kids where they can and can not go ie setting boundaries.

If a loved child is abducted and you want to get help finding them, call 1-800-THE-LOST(843-5678) or go to their website.
